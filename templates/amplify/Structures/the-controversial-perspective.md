---
title: The Controversial Perspective
type: amplify-structure
created: 2026-02-28
---

Be polarizing and controversial by stating a belief that a large percentage of people would disagree with. Challenge the status quo. Works well for video content designed to go viral because it attracts comments and engagement.

Being direct builds trust faster than being diplomatic. State the uncomfortable truth, then show why it helps. People respect honesty when it comes with evidence and a genuine desire to serve.

## Structure

1. Directly state the controversial perspective in one sentence. No hedging.
2. Ask a question that challenges the common belief system
3. Explain how the common belief system kept you stuck in some way
4. Tell the story of how your life got better when you changed your perspective
5. Explain why your perspective has more merit than the common way of seeing things
6. Describe the benefit to the viewer (why are they better off believing this?)

## Example

"If you don't make an effort to become wealthy, you're selfish. Please tell me, how are you going to truly make a difference in this world if you have no resources at your disposal? I used to believe it was selfish to pursue money, until I was broke and couldn't support my family, then my views changed. Money is a powerful tool that can be used for good or evil. But if you're a good person, which I'm sure you are, then it is selfish of you to not make more money, because you're the very kind of person who would do great things with it."

## Persuasion at Work

- **Controversy**: State your position without hedging, then back it with evidence. Weak opinions get ignored; strong ones with receipts get shared.
- **Overcoming Skepticism**: Anticipate the top 3 objections and address them before the audience raises them. This shows you have thought it through, not just reacted.
